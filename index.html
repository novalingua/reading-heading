Reading - Matching Heading

Basics

  • Matching Heading is an idea type of questions
  • Focused on main ideas, skimming
  • Tips
    • All parts of the chosen Heading must be correct
    • Practice First-Last Sentence extensively
    • Read List of Headings before the passage
    • Try to guess or predict what the passage is about
  • While skimming:
    • Figure out the organization of ideas
      • Does the passage become more general, or more specific?
      • Which paragraph contains lots of details?
